The tech

How LeakGuard works

A cloud-connected device attaches to the property's water meter. It runs on its own cellular connection -- no WiFi, no broadband, no dependency on the property's internet.

It learns the property's normal usage patterns and sends instant phone alerts when something looks wrong. Overnight flow, sudden spikes, slow creeping increases -- all flagged automatically.

The owner doesn't need to be present for installation. We coordinate with you or the keyholder and handle everything on site.
