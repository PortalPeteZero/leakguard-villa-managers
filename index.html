What LeakGuard does

A cloud-connected device sits on the property's water meter and monitors consumption 24/7. It runs on its own cellular connection -- no WiFi, no broadband, no dependency on the property's internet. It learns normal usage patterns and sends instant phone alerts when something looks wrong.

But it's not just about leaks. LeakGuard gives you a complete picture of water usage at every property:

  • Overnight flow -- water running when the property should be empty
  • Passing toilets -- a cistern trickling silently for weeks
  • Taps and hoses left on -- guest checks out, garden hose still running
  • Pool leaks -- weekly consumption trending up tells the story
  • Daily and hourly usage -- know what's happening without being there
Real data from a LeakGuard-monitored property -- daily usage with daytime and overnight flow breakdown.
